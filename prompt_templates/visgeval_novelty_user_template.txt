Evaluation Criteria:
Relevance (1-5) - selection of novel content for the summary. The image should include novel information for the summary. Annotators were instructed to penalize images which contained information that is already included in the summary.

Evaluation Steps:
1. Read the summary carefully and understand the main points discussed.
2. Look at the image description and understand what information it is conveying.
3. Compare the information in the image with the information in the summary.

5. If the image provides information that is already mentioned in the summary, rate it low on the novelty scale (1-2).
6. If the image provides some new information but also repeats some information from the summary, rate it in the middle of the novelty scale (3).
7. Make sure to consider the relevance of the new information provided by the image. If the new information is not relevant to the summary, it should not be considered as adding novelty.
